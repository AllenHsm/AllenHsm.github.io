Who Am I 关于我

Hello world! Here is Allen Hu, a computer science student at the University of California, Santa Barbara. As you can see, this blog is under construction and will improve gradually :-)

我叫Allen，出生在江南，后迁至深圳，现居圣塔芭芭拉——一座僻静安宁、美丽如画的海滨小城。

Reading 阅读

Reading is my biggest hobby. I believe that the god’s most precious gift to us is the ability to read and write.I enjoy reading.

Writing

Here I include coding into this topic. Writing, anyways, is a procedure of creating. I must admit that I am a poor writer, so I do not expect to publish my words. Rather, I choose to build up a blog, whose only reader is me myself, and ramble my life on it.

Traveling

Sometimes, I feel like I am a “footless” bird. Traveling and living in different places is another way to enrich my life.

What to expect here 初衷

Record my life and self-recognized progress I make, including:

  • Reading notes 读书笔记
  • Travelogue 游记
  • Coding reflection 开发随想
  • Miscellaneous notes 琐记、碎碎念

不求华丽，但求真实。
